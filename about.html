About Sticky Notes App

What is Sticky Notes App?

Sticky Notes App is a simple yet powerful tool to help you organize your thoughts, ideas, and tasks. With its intuitive interface and colorful notes, staying organized has never been easier!

Features

    Create and manage colorful sticky notes
    Organize tasks with due dates and priorities
    Customize note colors to categorize your thoughts
    Data saved locally in your browser
    Clean and intuitive interface

How to Use

Using Sticky Notes App is simple. Create new notes, edit them on the fly, and customize their colors to organize your thoughts. Your notes are automatically saved as you type.
